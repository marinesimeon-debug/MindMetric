Anxiety Disorders

Epidemiology: 301.4M Cases The most prevalent mental health category globally. Includes GAD, Panic Disorder, and Phobias.
Neuro-Biological Core Amygdala hyperactivity coupled with Prefrontal Cortex (PFC) hypo-connectivity, leading to failed emotion regulation.
Somatic Manifestation Muscle tension, autonomic arousal (sweating, heart rate variability), and chronic sleep onset latency.
Psychologist CBT Therapist

Eating Disorders

Clinical Spectrum Includes Anorexia Nervosa, Bulimia Nervosa, and Binge Eating Disorder (BED).
Psychosomatic Core Complex interaction between body image perception, reward circuitry (Dopamine), and emotional regulation.
Mortality Rate Anorexia has one of the highest mortality rates of any psychiatric condition due to medical complications.
Dietitian Psychotherapist

Clinical Depression

Impact: Leading Disability Affects ~280M people. Primary cause of suicide (700k deaths annually).
Neurotransmitter Imbalance Decreased monoamine availability (Serotonin, Norepinephrine, Dopamine) in the synaptic cleft.
Cognitive Decline Impairment in processing speed, attention, and executive function.
Psychiatrist

Anger & Impulse

IED (Intermittent Explosive Disorder) Recurrent, impulsive, aggressive outbursts that are disproportionate to the stressor.
Neurological Basis Low serotonin levels in the orbitofrontal cortex and anterior cingulate cortex.

Diagnosis Marker:

Abrupt onset of aggressive episodes lasting less than 30 minutes.

Neuropsychologist

Bipolar Disorder

Mood Oscillations Alternating between Mania/Hypomania (elevated energy) and severe Depressive episodes.
Genetic Heritability One of the most heritable conditions (estimated 80-85%).

Schizophrenia

Positive Symptoms Delusions, hallucinations (mostly auditory), and disorganized thinking.
Negative Symptoms Social withdrawal, apathy, and cognitive blunting.
Neuro-Psychiatrist

Addictive Behavior

Neuroplasticity Dopamine flooding leads to downregulation of D2 receptors, creating profound tolerance.
The Cravings Circuit Compulsive seeking behavior driven by the Nucleus Accumbens and Amygdala.

ADHD Spectrum

Executive Deficits Impoverished dopamine/norepinephrine signaling in the prefrontal cortex.
Symptom Profile Inattention, emotional dysregulation, and hyperactivity.
Pediatric Psychiatrist

PTSD / Trauma

Flashbacks & Re-experiencing Maladaptive consolidation of traumatic memories in the hippocampus.
Avoidance Patterns Avoiding internal or external cues associated with the trauma.
EMDR Therapist
