Epidemiological Landscape

Anxiety Disorders

Pathological fear and HPA axis hyper-reactivity.
Neurobiology
Amygdala hyperactivity and Prefrontal Cortex (PFC) hypo-connectivity. Dysregulation of GABA/Glutamate balance.

OCD

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder: The CSTC Circuit failure.
The CSTC Loop
Involves the Cortico-Striato-Thalamo-Cortical circuit. Intrusive thoughts (obsessions) trigger ritualistic acts (compulsions) to neutralize anxiety.
ERP Therapy
Serotonergic Focus

Borderline PD

Emotional instability and interpersonal hypersensitivity.
Emotional Dysregulation
Marked by "splitting" (black-and-white thinking) and intense fear of abandonment. Linked to reduced volume in the Hippocampus and Amygdala.
DBT Specialist
Personality Disorder

Autism Spectrum

Neurodevelopmental diversity in social communication.
Synaptic Connectivity
Theory of "Local Over-connectivity" and "Long-range Under-connectivity" in the brain. Focus on sensory processing and repetitive behaviors.

Eating Disorders

Anorexia, Bulimia, and Binge Eating (BED).
Insular Cortex Function
Distorted body image linked to dysregulation in the Insular Cortex and the Dopaminergic reward system related to starvation or purging.
Medical Nutrition
Psychosomatic

Sleep-Wake Disorders

Insomnia, Narcolepsy, and Circadian disruptions.
Circadian Rhythm
Melatonin signaling failure and Orexin/Hypocretin deficit (in Narcolepsy). Chronic insomnia is a major catalyst for MDD and Anxiety.
Somnologist
Melatonin Research

Major Depression

Monoamine deficiency and DLPFC hypo-activity.
Marker
Anhedonia and psychomotor retardation.
