Anxiety Disorders

Quantitative Data: 301 Million Cases Research confirms anxiety as the leading psychiatric condition globally. 75% of clinical cases are identified before the age of 24.
Clinical Markers Persistent cognitive fog, somatization (tachycardia), and maladaptive avoidant behaviors.

Clinical Depression

Economic Impact: $1 Trillion Annual Loss MDD affects 280 million people, representing the leading cause of global disability adjusted life years.
Primary Symptoms Anhedonia, disrupted circadian rhythms, and executive function deficit.

Specialists Recommended:

Psychiatrist (MD) Psychotherapist

Anger & Impulse

Neurological Basis: Amygdala Overdrive Weakened synaptic connectivity between the prefrontal cortex (control) and the amygdala.
Clinical Indicators Intermittent Explosive Disorder (IED) markers: rapid heart rate and verbal aggression.

Intervention Path:

Neuropsychologist Anger Specialist

Bipolar Disorder

Demographics: 40 Million People Spectrum disorder characterized by extreme shifts in activity levels.
Symptomatic Phases Mania alternating with severe depressive episodes.
Psychiatrist (Mood Specialist)

Schizophrenia

Global Prevalence: 24 Million A severe disorder involving hallucinations and disorganized thinking patterns.
Neurological Basis Dopamine D2 receptor sensitivity and grey matter volume alterations.

Mandatory Clinical Care:

Neuro-Psychiatrist Social Worker

Addiction & Reward

Substance Use: 35.6 Million The neurobiology of addiction centers on the hijacking of the Mesolimbic Dopamine System.
Predictive Risk High correlation with early-life trauma and genetic vulnerability.
