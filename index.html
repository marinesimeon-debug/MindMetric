Key Research Findings

Global Impact: Analyzing data from 29 countries shows that mental health is a major factor in overall public health, regardless of the economy.
Early Indicators: According to Harvard-based references, many symptoms begin to manifest before the age of 24.
Anxiety Focus: WHO data highlights anxiety as the most prevalent disorder, affecting millions across all demographics.
The Goal: By centralizing these diverse studies, we can create a clearer map for student awareness.
