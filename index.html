Addiction & Reward

Chronic dysfunction of the brain's reward, motivation, and memory systems.

Global Impact: 35 Million Disorders NIDA reports indicate 35 million people suffer from substance use disorders globally. Digital dependencies are currently rising at an accelerated rate.
Symptom Profile: Impulse control loss, tolerance development, and prioritize the stimuli over life-sustaining activities.
Predictive Analytics: Unaddressed addiction leads to permanent neuroplasticity alterations in the nucleus accumbens.
Sources: NIH / National Institute on Drug Abuse (NIDA)

Specialized Care Pathway:

Addictologist Psychiatrist (MD) CBT Specialist

Anxiety Disorders

Stats: 301 Million Cases 75% of clinical anxiety disorders solidify before the age of 24, making early campus intervention critical.
Symptomatic Markers: Cognitive fog, persistent tachycardia, and systemic avoidant behaviors.
Source: The Lancet Psychiatry / WHO Global health estimates

Specialists to consult:

Psychologist (CBT) Sophrologist

Clinical Depression

Economic Burden: $1 Trillion GDP Depression remains the leading cause of disability globally with 280 million clinical cases.
Key Symptoms: Anhedonia, lethargy, and significant executive function deficit.
Source: Harvard Medical School / WHO mhGAP

Medical Referral:

Psychiatrist Clinical Psychologist
