Anxiety Disorders

Excessive fear and autonomic dysregulation (301.4M cases).
Etiology & Neurobiology
Dysregulation of the HPA Axis and hypersensitivity of the Amygdala. Involves a failure in GABAergic inhibition and Glutamate over-excitation.
Somatic Triad
Includes persistent rumination (cognitive), tachycardia (physiological), and avoidant behavior (behavioral).
Psychologist CBT Therapist

Clinical Depression

MDD: Persistent state of low mood and cognitive slowing.
Neuro-Anatomical Markers
Associated with reduced hippocampal volume and decreased synaptic density in the Dorsolateral Prefrontal Cortex (DLPFC).
Monoamine Hypothesis
Functional deficiency of Serotonin, Norepinephrine, and Dopamine leading to profound anhedonia and lethargy.
Psychiatrist

Anger & Impulse

IED: Disproportionate aggressive outbursts (Intermittent Explosive Disorder).
Neurological Basis
Low serotonin levels in the orbitofrontal cortex and poor top-down control of the limbic system.
Diagnostic Marker
Abrupt episodes (onset < 30 mins) with a failure to resist aggressive impulses.
Neuropsychologist

Bipolar Spectrum

Oscillating energy states between Mania and Depression.
Cyclic Dynamics
Divided into Type I (Mania) and Type II (Hypomania). Characterized by "pressured speech" and hyper-excitability.
Genetic Heritability
Heritability rate of 80-85%. Linked to calcium signaling (CACNA1C) mutations.

Schizophrenia

Psychotic disruptions of thought, perception, and emotion.
Dopamine Hypothesis
Mesolimbic overactivity (positive symptoms) vs. Mesocortical underactivity (negative symptoms).
Cognitive Disorganization
Includes anosognosia (lack of insight) and fragmented logical associations.
Neuro-Psychiatrist

Addictive Behavior

Compulsive engagement despite harmful consequences.
Reward Circuitry
Dopaminergic flooding in the Nucleus Accumbens leading to receptor downregulation (tolerance).
Neuroplasticity
Permanent alteration of the "wanting" system vs. the "liking" system.

ADHD Spectrum

Neurodevelopmental deficit in executive functions.
Executive Deficits
Impoverished catecholamine signaling (Dopamine/NE) in the prefrontal cortex.
Pediatric Psychiatrist

PTSD / Trauma

Maladaptive memory consolidation following extreme stress.
Hippocampal Impact
Chronic cortisol elevation leading to memory fragmentation and re-experiencing (flashbacks).
EMDR Specialist
