Anxiety Disorders

Stats: 301 Million Cases 75% of cases solidify before age 24.
Common Symptoms: Palpitations, persistent "brain fog", avoidant behavior, and sleep disturbances (insomnia).
Predictive Risk: Can predict chronic cardiovascular issues and social isolation if left untreated.

Specialist to see:

Psychologist (CBT Specialist) Sophrologist

"Anxiety is a systemic response to uncertainty."

Clinical Depression

Impact: $1 Trillion GDP Loss Leading cause of disability worldwide (280 million people).
Common Symptoms: Anhedonia (loss of pleasure), extreme fatigue, changes in appetite, and suicidal ideation.
Predictive Risk: Long-term cognitive decline and increased risk of comorbid physical illnesses.

Specialist to see:

Psychiatrist (MD) Clinical Psychologist

Anger & Impulse

Neurobiology: Amygdala-Cortex disconnection leading to Intermittent Explosive Disorder (IED).
Common Symptoms: Sudden verbal/physical outbursts, "racing heart" during conflict, and immediate regret post-episode.
Predictive Risk: Predicts high risk of substance abuse and professional instability.

Specialist to see:

Neuropsychologist Behaviorist

Bipolar Disorder

Scale: 40 Million Cases Energy and mood regulation spectrum.
Common Symptoms: Alternating phases of Mania (hyperactivity, no sleep) and Depression (total withdrawal).
Predictive Risk: High vulnerability to financial risk-taking and severe mood destabilization.

Specialist to see:

Psychiatrist (Mood Specialist)

Essential: Mood stabilizers and long-term medical follow-up.
